About Me

Hi, I'm Jenny! I'm an international student from the US studying Physics and Computer Science. I'm especially interested in quantum computing. I love classical music and have been playing the viola for 11 years! At UofT, I am in the Skule Orchestra and 2 string quartets: Iron Strings and the Vivo String Quartet. I am also a peer mentor for the Computer Science Second Year Learning Community! I have worked in retail for a few years at Forever 21 and Anthropologie and have coached young musicians at Chamber Music Central.
